Hot oil Treatment

A Hot Oil Treatment is a deep conditioning method where natural, plant-based oils (like coconut or olive oil) are warmed and applied to the hair and scalp for intense nourishment and hydration.

Benefits:

  • Deep Hydration & Frizz Reduction: It replenishes lost moisture in dry, brittle hair, effectively sealing the hair cuticle to smooth the strands and significantly reduce frizz.
  • Scalp Health & Circulation: Massaging the warm oil stimulates blood flow to the scalp, which nourishes hair follicles and helps alleviate common issues like dryness and dandruff.
  • Increased Strength & Shine: The oil strengthens the hair shaft from within, minimizing breakage and split ends, and leaving the hair with a healthy, vibrant shine.
  • Damage Repair: Hot oil is highly beneficial for chemically damaged or over-processed hair (e.g., color-treated or relaxed hair) as it fills in the porous gaps in the hair structure.
