Volume Boost Mousse

Volume Boost Mousse is a foamy, airy styling product that is applied to damp hair before blow-drying or air-drying.

Benefits:

  • Creates Root Lift and Body: It effectively lifts hair at the roots and plumps up the individual strands to deliver noticeable, all-over volume and fullness.
  • Provides Flexible Hold: It offers a light to medium hold that keeps your style in place while leaving the hair feeling soft and natural, not crunchy or stiff.
  • Enhances Styling Longevity: By giving the hair structure, it helps the styled shape last much longer, preventing the hair from falling flat quickly.
  • Heat Protection: Many modern formulas contain ingredients that offer thermal protection, safeguarding the hair against damage during blow-drying.

Directions:

Shake the can well, then dispense an egg-sized dollop of mousse into your palm and distribute it evenly across your damp hair.
